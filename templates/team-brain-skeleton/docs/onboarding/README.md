# Onboarding — TEAM-NAME

Welcome. This is the team brain. Here's what you do in your first week.

## Day 1

- Clone the team-brain repo.
- Run `teammate init` (one-time per laptop).
- Read `CLAUDE.md`, the rules in `.claude/rules/`, and `knowledge/services.md`.
- Ask `teammate ask "what does my team do?"` to see the local LLM citing
  the brain.

## Day 2-5

- Read the runbooks in `docs/runbooks/`. Skim the ADRs in `knowledge/decisions/`.
- Pair with someone on call. Ask the brain whenever you're stuck.
- Open your first PR — even just adding a typo fix to `docs/onboarding/`
  to learn the flow.

## Week 2+

- Notice tribal knowledge that's NOT in the brain. Add it. Open a PR.
  The brain gets smarter every time someone writes down what they used
  to keep in their head.
